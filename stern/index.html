Headed for a career in Science, Technology, Engineering, or Math?

The STEM to Stern program is a one-year class for accelerating the growth of students in the science, technology, engineering, and math fields. You will learn a variety of skills as wells as accelerate your professional growth.

Benefits include:

Excel in Science and Math cources

Learn STEM smarts as well as pick the teacher you love with Priority Registration

Build professional skills

Learn how to network, interview, and build a resume.

Meet awesome mentors

Past mentors include professionals from NOAA, Microsoft, Boeing, and more!

po

Get Internships!

STEM to Stern students have interned at Adobe, the Pacific Northwest National Laboratories, Puget Sound Energy, Costco, Symetra, and many other organizations.
